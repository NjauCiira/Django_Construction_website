ABOUT US

Blue Point helps our customers deliver projects of purpose that create a lasting positive legacy. These are projects that create jobs and grow economies; improve the resiliency of the world's infrastructure; connect communities to resources and opportunity; get us closer to net zero; protect Kenya and allied interests; tackle critical environmental challenges to protect people and the planet; and accelerate progress to make the world a cleaner, greener, safer place. While expertise enables delivery, partnerships ensure long-term success, which is why we align everything we do to our customers’ goals. Since 1995, we have helped customers complete more than 2,500 projects in 32 countries in Africa.

We are approaching our Diversity and Inclusion (D&I) strategy with a new sense of urgency—not as an initiative, but for long-term sustainability, managing it with the same focus on results that we bring to our projects. We have organized our efforts into a

three-pillar action plan.

THREE PILLAR ACTION PLAN
TALENT

We will attract, engage, and retain a diverse workforce that reflects the makeup of society, our customers, and the communities where we live, work, and serve.

CULTURE

We will foster an inclusive, bias-free workplace aligned with our VV&Cs where everyone feels valued, respected, welcomed and treated fairly.

ACCOUNTABILITY

We will maintain a governance process, ensuring we employ best practices, and bring transparency and accountability to our challenges, plans, and progress toward meeting our D&I goals.
